Volleyball Rules
Six players on a team, 3 on the front row and 3 on the back row. Maximum of three hits per side. Player may not hit the ball twice in succession (A block is not considered a hit). Ball may be played off the net during a volley and on a serve. A ball hitting a boundary line is in. A ball is out if it hits an antennae, the floor completely outside the court, any of the net or cables outside the antennae, the referee stand or pole, the ceiling above a nonplayable area. It is legal to contact the ball with any part of a players body. It is illegal to catch, hold, or throw the ball.
